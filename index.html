03 / Behind the tabs

A LITTLE
ABOUT ME.

The professional bio, but with the margins left in.

Hi, I'm Sree.

I’m a multidisciplinary creative who never quite fit into one box — so I stopped trying. My work moves between strategy, storytelling and visuals: social media one day, a film frame the next, a piece of writing after that, and an AI experiment somewhere in between.

I like ideas with personality, work with texture, and portfolios that don’t feel like corporate waiting rooms.

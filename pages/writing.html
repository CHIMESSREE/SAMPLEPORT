Film / Analysis

Collar × Malice

2023 · Japanese · Thriller · 59m
“Well paced” — Sree

Synopsis Collar × Malice follows a rookie police officer Ichika Hoshino stationed in Shinjuku, Tokyo.

On her way home from work, she is attacked and a collar with poison is placed around her neck; following this, she becomes involved with the "X-Day Incident", a string of murders done by a terrorist group called Adonis. Under the threat of death, Ichika is forced to investigate the X-Day Incident together with a group of former police officers.

Genre: Visual Novel, Thriller, Suspense, Crime, Animation Year of Release: May and June 2023.

No. of Episodes: 2 Part film Duration: 59 mins 37 seconds

Overall feel of the anime

The film is very engaging and has good quality sound and background scores to help one enjoy while watching. It is a well paced film with right time of plot twists and suspense creation. The characters are also beautiful, making one to love each and every character, even the villain.

Story and Narration The story is well scripted and has the right amount of twists and turns that would keep one thinking of the next move. The film is visualized in a proper format and flow which won't confuse the audience or the viewers and have a smooth sailing experience watching the whole film.

The anime watchers will for sure love it but the general audience will also want to watch it.

Animation in all

The animation is close to accurate and is of 4k quality but there are frames where the animation isn't as smooth as it is supposed to be. Only the frame and character movements lack smoothness which won't be noticed by the general audience.

You love every character — even the villain.

But the characters are designed amazingly and there is detailing in the animation which is of utmost importance for the one that is of the suspense and crime genre.

Sound and Background scores

The sounds in the animation is one way the viewers can be kept captivated and this anime has really good sound effects with a good amount of volume.

There are no background scores as such but the opening and closing music of the anime are foot tapping good and with good bass and volume. The opening and closing are again an important aspect in anime that reminds the audience of a certain anime they watched.

Should we acquire the anime?

Yes of course, and it will be a good buy.

Is the anime streaming on any other platforms?

No. But on anime websites.

Record Without Words

2023 · Mandarin · Psychological Thriller
“Quietly brilliant” — Sree

Plot

  • The film revolves around Li LiZhong who lost his father and is placed as the captain in the security force of a village, then he is exposed to the dark secrets of his own family.
  • The story is very artsy and more of a Thriller Drama than Action Thriller.
  • Slow flow of the film and events won't be accepted by the general public.
  • The film is a great Psychological Thriller Action Drama.
  • Even being slow the film keeps the audience interested and engaged till the end because of the secret it reveals gradually.

Cinematography

The film has amazing cinematography and direction.

The shot where the character "Chen" gives the reason of why he murdered all the white dogs in the village he talks about a human being there, that was shot with a psychological aspect of someone mentally disabled.

The night shots are also properly shot.

Execution/Direction

The execution is done really well that makes the complex story easy for the audience to understand.

The scene especially where the lead actor is shown being revealed of his father's dark secrets and how the lead actor connects the dots, this helps the audience connect the story too.

Dialogues

The subtitles are quite precise and not exaggerated, which helps understand every bit of the story.

The execution makes a complex story easy to follow.

Pace

The pace of the film can be a problem as it is quite slow and can make few of them lose interest in the film.

VFX, sound & Lights

The sound in the film is clear, loud and matches the situation. The lights are also used accurately in the dark scenes especially that keeps the film visible.

Suggestion

The film has a really good plot and the execution is proper as well. But it is a little artsy and slow, which can be a hindrance.

The Eye Behind the Wall

2023 · Sci-fi · Mystery
“Almost” — Sree

THE EYE BEHIND THE WALL

Plot

The film has a story quite similar to the anime "Attack on Titan".

The story and the concept is quite intriguing but the film lacks so many sci-fi elements that the audience would expect to watch.

There could have been more glitches, creatures, man in black, spaceships, futuristic objects.

Because the film looks quite big budget to be able to indulge the elements in the film.

The film gradually opens to the actual plot and the mystery in the film.

The film contains too much nudity and sexual content.

One there is the couple's intimate nude scenes and then there is a part where the female lead has to make a nude video with the stranger to cross the border in the film which is shown clearly as well.

Cinematography

The film is directed fantabulously. Though the film looks long and stretchy it is clearly understood and every plot is explained.

Dialogues

The conversations between the characters are quite precise and unnecessarily elaborate.

An intriguing concept it never quite pays off.

Pace

The pace of the film is quite slow and it lacks so many scenes that are not edited out but are unnecessary.

VFX, Sound & Lights

Nothing sort of VFX is used much. The ones used were something that could be done by a kid too.

The sound effects are good but more was expected.

The lights are perfectly used to enhance or add a gloomy effect in the film.

The colour grading of the film is good too.

All in all the film could have been better. I expected a little more. As the film ended on a cliff without a hanger.

Suggestion

The film as a Sci-fi is good to watch but there is a bit too much nudity and the pace is a little slow.

War on Terror

2023 · Malay · Action · Crime
“Rich, grounded” — Sree

Title

Language: Malay, English Subs Year of Release: 23 November 2023 Film Review

Overall feel of the film

The film has us on an adrenaline rush. The story escalates gradually that helps the viewers to slowly sink in every scene and understand where the ride headed to. The film has good characters and the ones that are actually necessary. No character seems like an outcast.

The film has a protagonist who is rebellious and then a side character to balance it. The story flows very smoothly and has a good chronology of events except for a place at 44:00 to 48:31 Rest the movie has a good impact especially for the audiences who have seen such events happen in front of them.

Production Value

The production is rich as the film has used enough equipment in the action sequences.

The film has scenes of bombardment, shootings, car explosions, rope ways, etc. It also is made very realistically which can be seen with the number of people they have used in the film to show the crowd.

The Art Direction is also on point that has portrayed the hides of the terrorists.

Narrative

The story is flowing in a smooth way and there are no unnecessary flashbacks or multiple POVs at a time to confuse the audience. The end credit scene that shows the main player in the whole plan was rightly set. Even though there is a protagonist, other characters are also given equal part in the whole story leaving no one behind.

Pace

The film is well paced and escalates gradually maintaining its speed and that is what helps the story be in the mind. Time gap is given to the viewer to soak in every bit of the scenes before entering the next turn.

Location

The locations are chosen wisely. The places that seem quite crowded and the mid city area are chosen to show bombardments, while the hiding places are selected where it may seem impossible for terrorists to attack. The shooting scenes were shot in daylight and in the corridors of a building that would make it look real.

Story

The synopsis in IMDb can be misleading as the protagonist and life are not the very core of the film but the everyday life of a military officer and the people he is surrounded by while at work and the run to save the country.

The story is first thought to be very simple, but that is the very jist of it. Without the unnecessary over exaggeration of action scenes, it's kept real to align with the true events.

Characters

The characters are wisely placed. There are no characters that would seem useless.

The story beneath the headline story.

Each character has their own struggles which is briefly shown as the film seems to be part 1 of the sequel.

Technical aspects like: Cinematography, Lighting, Sound, VFX, etc.

  • The cinematography is quite on point. The shots are taken very systematically and make it seem real.
  • The lighting is so accurate that the location even after being a simple place has the vibes that the frame of the film wants the viewer to feel.
  • The sound quality is really good and has enough beats and BGM needed which keeps the audience interested.
  • The VFX used to show bombardment is very accurate and makes it seem real.

Especially the scene where Zack is tied up with a bomb and the blast looks very real.

Additional observations, if any

The actors playing Joe seem to be over-acting but that is to show that they are psychopaths and don't bother killing innocent people.

At 44:00 to 48:31 where the villain seems to be captured and under their control in 44 mins but is out meeting his father at 48:31.

Why should we acquire this movie?

Yes, of course Why should we acquire it even if it is old?

It's not old but yet to be released The film keeps the audience belted to seats till the very last to know the next move and understand the whole events. It is an amazing action thriller about the military and police in Malaysia and how they fight with the inborne terrorists and naxals.

Is the film already streaming on OTTs, if yes – Name of OTT, language / subtitles, etc.

No it's yet to release Should we consider / buy or skip the film?

Must buy and it will be a good purchase considering the audience we have who like the action content

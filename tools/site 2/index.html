Every child deserves a seat in school, not a shift at work.

Ujala Foundation finds out-of-school children in industrial towns and salt-pan settlements, gets them enrolled, and stays with them until they finish Class 10. Simple mission. Stubborn follow-through.

Three jobs. Done properly.

We don't run fifty programmes. We run three, and we measure all of them child by child, name by name.

Find

Doorstep Surveys

Field teams map every settlement in our districts twice a year, identifying children who have dropped out or never enrolled.

Enrol

Back-to-School Bridge

Six-month bridge classes bring children up to grade level, then we walk them — literally — to their government school admission.

Stay

Retention Circles

Community mentors, after-school study rooms, and monthly parent circles keep children in class through Class 10.

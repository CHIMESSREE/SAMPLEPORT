What we found

Where the account stands today

A quick read of the last ninety days, before we propose anything.

  • 1
    Posting is reactive, not plannedActivity spikes around events and goes quiet for weeks in between, so the account never builds momentum with the algorithm or with parents.
  • 2
    Strong material, flat presentationThe photography from annual day and sports meet is genuinely good, but it's posted raw — no crop discipline, no captions doing any work.
  • 3
    Nothing speaks to prospective parentsThe feed documents events for people who already attend. There's very little that answers what an admission-season parent is actually asking.
  • 4
    Reels are untappedReach on the three reels posted last term was roughly nine times that of static posts, and they haven't been repeated since.
